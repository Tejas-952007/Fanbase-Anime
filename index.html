Welcome to the world of Attack on Titan!

Join the fight for humanity's survival against the Titans. Explore characters, episodes, and the epic story that has captivated fans worldwide.

Scout Regiment (Survey Corps)

The Scout Regiment is responsible for exploring areas outside the walls and fighting Titans directly. They are the bravest soldiers, risking their lives to protect humanity and uncover the truth about the world. Although they suffer heavy casualties, the Scouts play the most important role in pushing humanity toward freedom.

Garrison Regiment

The Garrison Regiment protects the walls and cities from Titan attacks. Their main duty is to guard the gates, repair wall damage, and use cannons to defend civilians. They are less involved in exploration but are essential for maintaining safety inside the walls.

Military Police Regiment

The Military Police Regiment maintains law and order within the inner cities, especially inside Wall Sina. They protect the royal family and enforce the government's rules. While they live safer lives than other soldiers, they are often criticized for corruption and avoiding direct combat.
