The Story So Far

From the fall of Wall Maria to the Rumbling. Here is the breakdown of the legendary timeline, hidden secrets, and fan theories.

Season 1: The Fall

Humanity lives in fear inside three walls. But when a Colossal Titan kicks a hole in Wall Maria, the illusion of peace is shattered. Eren Yeager watches his mother get eaten and swears to kill every single Titan. The nightmare begins here.

⚠ Hidden Intel (Spoiler): Did you notice the steam coming from Annie Leonhart early in the season? The clues that she was the Female Titan were there from the very beginning.

Season 2: Betrayal

The Titans are inside the walls! As the Scouts scramble to uncover the breach, a shocking truth is revealed: The enemies are hiding amongst them. The reveal of the Armored and Colossal Titans is one of the most iconic moments in anime history.

⚠ Hidden Intel (Spoiler): The Ending Song of Season 2 ("Yuugure no Tori") actually showed the entire history of Ymir and the Eldians years before it was revealed in the anime!

Season 3: The Basement

It's not just Titans anymore; it's humans vs. humans. The Scouts overthrow the corrupt government and finally retake Wall Maria. They reach The Basement, discovering that the world is not what they thought. They are not the last humans... they are devils to the rest of the world.

⚠ Hidden Intel (Spoiler): The title of Episode 1 is "To You, in 2000 Years." In Season 3, we learn about the curse of Ymir, connecting the timeline back to the very start.

The Final Season: War

4 years later. The battlefield shifts to Marley. Eren is no longer the victim; he is the attacker. Lines between good and evil are blurred as the Rumbling begins. The fight for freedom becomes a fight for genocide.

⚠ Hidden Intel (Spoiler): Eren's eyes change color throughout the season, symbolizing the different Titan powers (Founding vs Attack) conflicting within him.

Famous Fan Theories

The Time Loop Theory

Many fans believe Eren is stuck in a time loop, trying to find a future where his friends survive. The first words "See you later, Eren" by Mikasa hint that this has happened before.

The "Akuma no Ko" Theory

Based on the ending song, this theory suggests that Eren accepted he had to become the "Child of the Devil" to unite the world against him, a sacrificial "Code Geass" style ending.

Ymir's Love

The theory that Ymir Fritz wasn't a slave to the King by force, but by a twisted form of love, which is why she waited 2000 years for Mikasa (who could kill the one she loved) to free her.
