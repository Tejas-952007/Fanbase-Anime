Main Characters

Eren Yeager

The main protagonist who swore to destroy all Titans. He possesses the power of the Attack Titan and the Founding Titan, driving the fight for freedom.

Mikasa Ackerman

Eren's adoptive sister and the top-ranked soldier of the 104th Training Corps. She possesses immense physical strength and combat skills.

Armin Arlert

Eren's childhood friend. Though physically weaker, his strategic genius and intelligence have saved the Scouts countless times.

Levi Ackerman

The Captain of the Special Operations Squad. Known as "Humanity's Strongest Soldier," he is legendary for his speed and deadly precision.

Erwin Smith

The 13th Commander of the Survey Corps. A visionary leader willing to sacrifice everything, including his own humanity, for the sake of victory.

Reiner Braun

A graduate of the 104th Cadet Corps who holds a dark secret. He possesses the power of the Armored Titan.
